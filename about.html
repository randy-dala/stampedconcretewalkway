Our Story

Northeast Decorative Concrete started the way a lot of New Hampshire businesses start — with a skill, a truck, and a handshake. Over four decades ago, our founder fell in love with the art and science of decorative concrete. At the time, stamped concrete was a niche specialty most contractors didn't offer. He saw an opportunity: bring this beautiful, durable alternative to natural stone to New Hampshire homeowners at a fair price.

What began as a one-man operation working out of Concord grew into the region's premier decorative concrete company. Along the way, the business became a family affair — knowledge passed down, standards raised, and a reputation built one patio, one handshake, and one satisfied customer at a time.

Today, we're still family-owned, still based in Concord, and still driven by the same principle that started it all: every project should be something we're proud to put our name on.
