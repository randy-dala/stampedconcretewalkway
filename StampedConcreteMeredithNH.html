Meredith's Trusted Lakes Region Stamped Concrete Contractor

Meredith is the jewel of the Lakes Region — a Belknap County community of about 6,400 residents perched on Meredith Bay at the northern tip of Lake Winnipesaukee. Home to the iconic Mill Falls at the Lake resort complex, Meredith Village, and Lake Waukewan, this charming town blends resort-town elegance with year-round New England community.

We've completed projects throughout Meredith — from the waterfront estates on Meredith Bay and Meredith Neck to the established neighborhoods along Main Street, Waukewan Street, Route 25, and Pleasant Street. We also serve the lakefront homes on Lake Waukewan and Wicwas Lake.

Meredith's terrain features steep lakefront slopes, sandy glacial soils, and granite ledge on higher elevations. Waterfront properties must comply with Shoreland regulations, while hillside lots require retaining solutions. Our Lakes Region expertise ensures every project is engineered perfectly for its site.

Case Study

Terracing a 60-Foot Slope on Meredith Neck

A Meredith Neck homeowner wanted a pool, patio, and dock connection — but the property drops 60 feet from the house to the Winnipesaukee shoreline. The steep grade, Shoreland regulations, and exposed granite ledge made this one of our most technically challenging Lakes Region projects.

We designed a four-tier terraced system: an upper entertaining patio with outdoor kitchen at house level, a mid-level pool terrace with infinity-edge sight lines to the lake, a lower sun deck with Adirondack seating, and a final stepped path to the dock. Each terrace uses natural fieldstone patterns and retaining walls that blend with exposed ledge. Integrated LED pathway lighting illuminates the entire descent. The homeowner now flows from dinner to swim to sunset cruise seamlessly — all within Shoreland compliance.

Meredith Stamped Concrete FAQ

Common questions from Meredith homeowners.

Can you handle steep lakefront grades?

Absolutely! Steep lakefront slopes are our specialty in Meredith. We design terraced systems with retaining walls, stepped paths, and multi-level patios that navigate grades safely and beautifully — all within Shoreland limits.

What about Winnipesaukee Shoreland rules?

All Meredith properties within 250 feet of Lake Winnipesaukee, Waukewan, or Wicwas are subject to the NH Shoreland Act. We expertly design within impervious coverage limits and handle all permitting for lakefront installations.

What patterns suit lakefront resort properties?

Travertine and sandstone create resort-luxury feels. Fieldstone blends with natural lakefront. Bluestone suits village cottages. We bring samples for lakeside comparison.

Can you build dock connections?

Yes! Dock-connected paths with proper grading, lighting, and Shoreland compliance are one of our most popular Meredith services. We handle everything from house to waterline seamlessly.

Do you work with exposed granite ledge?

We incorporate exposed ledge as dramatic design elements rather than fighting it. Our terracing integrates natural rock with stamped concrete for a seamless, organic lakefront aesthetic.
