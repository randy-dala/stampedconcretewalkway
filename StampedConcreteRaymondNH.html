Case Study

Navigating Shoreland Regulations at Onway Lake

An Onway Lake homeowner wanted a grand lakefront terrace but was limited by NH Shoreland Protection Act restrictions on impervious coverage within 250 feet of the lake. The allowed square footage was half of their dream patio, and traditional contractors told them it couldn't be done.

We designed a hybrid solution: a 400 sq ft stamped concrete terrace (within impervious limits) seamlessly connected to a permeable paver extension that doesn't count against the Shoreland allotment. The stamped section anchors the outdoor kitchen and fire pit, while the permeable extension provides the sun terrace and seating area. Both surfaces use matching sandstone-tone patterns, so the entire 700 sq ft space looks unified. We handled all DES permitting, and the inspector called it "the most creative Shoreland-compliant patio I've ever approved."

Raymond Stamped Concrete FAQ

Common questions from Raymond homeowners.

What about Shoreland Protection Act restrictions?

We're experts in NH Shoreland Act compliance. We design within impervious coverage limits and can create hybrid stamped concrete/permeable paver solutions that maximize your patio size while meeting all DES regulations.

Can you handle lakefront access paths?

Yes! We design stepped paths and terraced access routes from homes to lake shores with non-slip surfaces, proper grading, and shoreline-compatible construction.

What about sandy lakeside soils?

Raymond's sandy soils near Onway and Governors Lakes require deeper sub-bases with geotextile fabric. Our engineered foundations prevent settling and shifting on these loose soils.

Do you handle DES permitting?

Absolutely! We handle all NH DES Shoreland permits, wetland buffer compliance, and impervious coverage calculations. We make the permitting process seamless for lakefront homeowners.

What patterns work best near lakes?

Sandstone in golden tones echoes the shoreline. Flagstone in natural browns suits wooded lakefronts. Travertine in ivory creates a resort feel. We bring samples for lakeside comparison.
