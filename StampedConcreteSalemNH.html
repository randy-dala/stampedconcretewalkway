Salem's Trusted Stamped Concrete Contractor

Salem is southern NH's largest town with nearly 30,000 residents, a vibrant commercial corridor along Route 28/South Broadway, and beloved destinations like Canobie Lake Park. From the historic Depot district to the upscale neighborhoods of North Salem, Pelham Road, and Shadow Lake, Salem offers diverse residential styles and a booming economy adjacent to the Massachusetts border.

We've completed projects throughout Salem — from the executive homes on North Policy Street and Pelham Road to the lakefront properties around Canobie Lake, Shadow Lake, and Arlington Pond. We also serve the established neighborhoods on Lawrence Road, Cluff Road, and throughout the Depot district.

Case Study

Turning a Route 28 Parking Lot into a Dining Destination

A popular South Broadway restaurant needed outdoor dining to compete with the growing restaurant scene. Their challenge: converting a worn asphalt parking lot corner into an inviting patio that met Salem's commercial codes, ADA requirements, and the restaurant's upscale vision — all while keeping the remaining parking functional.

We demolished the asphalt section, engineered proper commercial-grade sub-base, and installed a European cobblestone patio with matching entrance walkway, integrated planters, decorative iron railing, and stanchion-post bollard lighting. The patio seats 40 guests with full ADA access and complies with all Salem commercial zoning. The restaurant saw a 55% increase in warm-weather revenue the first summer and earned Salem's Commercial Beautification Award.

Salem Stamped Concrete FAQ

Common questions from Salem homeowners and business owners.

Do you handle commercial projects?

Yes! Salem's busy commercial corridor is one of our specialties. We design commercial-grade patios, walkways, and building entrances that meet all Salem zoning codes, ADA requirements, and can handle heavy foot traffic.

What about lakefront regulations?

Canobie Lake, Shadow Lake, and Arlington Pond all have Shoreland Act restrictions. We're experts at maximizing your patio within impervious limits and handle all permitting.

Can you match my executive home's style?

Absolutely! North Salem's upscale homes deserve equally upscale outdoor spaces. We work with Italian slate, European ashlar, travertine, and custom patterns that complement any architectural style.

How does stamped concrete handle NH winters?

Our engineered sub-bases and commercial-grade sealers withstand Salem's freeze-thaw cycles. Proper installation and bi-annual resealing ensure decades of beautiful performance even with deicing salt exposure.

Do you work with Salem permits and zoning?

Yes! We're familiar with Salem's building permits, zoning regulations, and commercial codes. We handle permitting so you don't have to navigate the town hall process yourself.
